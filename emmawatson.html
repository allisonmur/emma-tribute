EMMA WATSON

Badass Feminist

She is the UN Women Goodwill Ambassador

In July 2014, Emma was appointed UN Women Goodwill Ambassador. Her goal is to empower young women and serve as an advocate for the UN women's HeForShe campaign which promotes gender equality. She has been involved in the promotion of women's education across the globe.

She started a book club

Emma Watson reads a lot especially about gender equality. She decided to start a book club to educate and inspire others through the recommendations of books shes read. There are close to 140,000 members! She has even had the opportunity to speak to a lot of the authors.
